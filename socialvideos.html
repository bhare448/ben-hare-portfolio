LVC Social Media Interviews

Videos that I was asked to edit during my internship.

Project Overview

During my internship, one of the main tasks that I was given for a large part of my time there was to go around the school with another student worker and interview faculty members asking them what they wanted to be when they were growing up and how they got to the position they are in now. I was not present to record the ones shown above, so I was instead asked to edit them. My supervisor gave me a rough storyboard and explained exactly what she wanted. I was nervous at first, as I had very little experience with video editing, but the student worker I was recording these videos with introduced me to the DaVinci Resolve editing software and taught me the basic functions. After that, I was able to learn the rest pretty quickly and ended up creating these videos without much trouble. I even found that I enjoyed editing videos and have played around with DaVinci Resolve a lot more since then.

Final Outcome

I am very glad to have been given this task, as it provided me with filming and sound experience, which is helpful knowledge to possess in my field. Additionally, it helped me discover a new hobby and something I would not mind pursuing more in the future. For my first video-based project, I am pleased with how it turned out.
