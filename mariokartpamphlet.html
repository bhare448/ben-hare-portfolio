Mario Kart 8 Pamphlet

Pamphlet that teaches players the basics of playing the video game "Mario Kart 8".

Project Overview

This was the final project made for my "Digital Graphic Design" class. The assignment was to make an educational pamphlet that teaches users how to perform a task step by step. The topic could be anything that we wanted, so I decided to make a pamphlet that teaches people how to play one of my favorite games, Mario Kart 8, because I felt that I was knowledgeable enough on the topic to teach it to others.

Design Process

Ideation:

  • I originally went through many different ideas on what to teach in my pamphlet since the ability to choose any topic meant the options were very broad. I knew that if I was going to do anything, I wanted it to be something that I would have fun with.
  • Since video games are my main hobby of choice, I figured that sharing information about my favorite games would be something that I have great interest in. Since Mario Kart is a very well-known franchise that most people have played at some point, it seemed like the perfect topic for me.

Research:

  • When looking at similar video game guides, I noticed that many of them include detailed walkthroughs and descriptions, but still focus heavily on the visuals with lots of artwork from the game.
  • The audience that collects game guides mostly enjoy them because of the visuals so I wanted to make sure I leaned in heavily on the aesthetics.

Brainstorming:

  • When deciding what kinds of information to include in the pamphlet, I did not want to make it too complex so that casual players would be able to understand it. I also wanted to include more than the bare minimum of information so that people can legitimately improve their skills by reading it.
  • The pamphlet was required to be a certain number of pages and include at least five steps. This ended up working out perfectly for me because I was able to fit all of the steps I had thought of into the pamphlet.

Prototype:

  • The layout of this pamphlet follows a similar layout to official video game guide books. I included offical artwork from the game on every page and separated each section using boxes.
  • I included 5 steps starting with the basic information of the characters, then explaining how each of the items worked, and teaching some more advanced techniques by the end.

Final Outcome

This project was an effective introduction to designing for a pamphlet. They require a much different mindset compared to designing for other forms of media because there is more limited space to work with. I'm happy that I was able to make it about a topic I was genuinely intereted in and I am happy with the final product.
